FloodSense

Smart Flood Early Warning

FloodSense protects your community from flood threats

FloodSense is a smart flood warning solution designed to protect communities, farms, and high-risk areas. With this system, any rise in water levels can be detected early and immediate warnings are sent to residents and relevant parties.

With FloodSense, every precious second can be used to save lives, property, and crops. It provides peace of mind to local communities, enabling quick action to be taken before situations become critical.

Early flood warnings directly to phones

Suitable installation in villages & farms

Features of FloodSense

Solar-Powered CCTV

24/7 surveillance camera powered by solar with AI-ready technology for real-time flood monitoring.

NB-IoT Water Level Sensor

Water level sensor with 4-meter range detection and NB-IoT communication for continuous monitoring.

Real-time Alerts

Immediate alert system through SMS, phone calls, and mobile applications to communities and authorities.

AI-Powered Analytics

Predictive AI analytics for flood forecasting based on weather data and historical water levels.

Government-Grade Infrastructure

Government-grade infrastructure for disaster management with 24/7 remote monitoring.

Mobile Accessibility

Easy access through mobile applications for management and monitoring from any location.
